About Us

Our Mission

Welcome to ATHMATHON, a 24-hour hackathon organized by AWH Engineering College as part of its techno-management fest, ATHMA24. We are a team of passionate individuals driven by a single mission: to empower students to bridge the gap between innovation and technology. We believe that the most impactful solutions stem from creative minds equipped with the right technical tools. ATHMATHON provides a platform to cultivate this approach by challenging participants to develop solutions to real-world problems within a limited timeframe.

Unleashing Problem-Solving

ATHMATHON is designed to ignite your passion for creative problem-solving and technical innovation. Here, you'll get to collaborate with fellow tech enthusiasts, brainstorm ideas, and translate them into tangible solutions. Join us on May 2nd and 3rd, 2024, and embark on a thrilling 24-hour journey where you'll push your boundaries and showcase your technical prowess. Stay tuned for more information about the specific challenge, registration process, and exciting prizes. We look forward to seeing you at ATHMATHON!

Features

Key Features

Challenge Your Skills

Brace yourself for a challenge! ATHMATHON will present you with a real-world problem that requires a creative and technical solution. You'll have 24 hours to collaborate with your team, brainstorm ideas, and translate them into a tangible solution. This is your opportunity to showcase your technical expertise, think outside the box, and develop innovative approaches to complex problems.

Foster Collaboration

Innovation thrives in collaboration! ATHMATHON is designed to bring together tech enthusiasts from diverse backgrounds. You'll have the chance to form a team, share your unique skillsets, and work together to create groundbreaking solutions. The collaborative environment fosters teamwork, communication, and the exchange of ideas. It's an exceptional platform to build connections and learn from your fellow participants.

Recognition and Rewards

Your hard work and ingenuity deserve recognition! At ATHMATHON, we'll be celebrating the most innovative and impactful solutions developed by the participating teams. Compete for exciting prizes and the chance to have your creation acknowledged by the tech community. The recognition you receive at ATHMATHON can be a major stepping stone in your technical journey.
